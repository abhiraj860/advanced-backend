PROCESS OF DEVELOPMENT

Our technological innovation process consists of a series of phases necessary to implement any model of development whether it is Agile Development or Waterfall Development.

  • 0 1.

    Analysis

    .

    In this stage, we check and analyze the whole project requirements of our client understanding the purpose of building the product, and accordingly, plan the entire procedure of making the work happen. We analyze the technological innovation process focused on meeting our client's needs. The analysis is the key component of our company.

  • 0 2.

    Requirements Specification

    .

    Here, our company requires a brief description of a software system that needs to be developed. The specification contains the information necessary for the design, verification, functionality, features, and purpose of a product that we going to build. Assumptions, Risks, and Dependencies. This is important for us to create the product of your needs

  • 0 3.

    Design

    .

    In this phase, we start working on your ideas to create your visualized product. Our specialty is that we mainly focus on UI and UX where we prepare a working prototype based on your ideas and wait for your approval. Once it is approved we focus on the entire user experience providing you a horizon of opportunities for your project

  • 0 4.

    Implementation

    .

    This is the sage where the solution is actually built. There's no success of any project without this crucial step "implementation". When reaching a solution to the client's need it is finally time to develop the product or services. Our team of specialized experts works especially to analyze the workflow and implementation process.

  • 0 5.

    Testing & Integration

    .

    Here, a level of software testing where individual units are combined and tested as a group. The purpose of this level of testing is to expose faults in the interaction between integrated units test drivers and test tubs that are used to assist in integration testing. We Run integration tests until something fails, Determine what needs to be added or modified, Drill down until the whole process is integrated.

  • 0 6.

    Operation & Maitenance

    .

    Once the process is done, we provide ongoing support with your specialized services and ensure that you face no trouble during your experience. Operation and maintenance (O&M) activities, which encompass not only technical issues but also managerial, social, and much more. We support you if you need help for the future development of a project, based on your ideas and input from users.
